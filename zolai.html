Mi a ZOLAI

Decision OS
Szabályokra, invariánsokra és guardokra épülő döntési rendszer, amely minden döntést explicit, visszakövethető formában kezel.

DETERMINISTIC GUARD-FIRST AUDIT-GRADE DECISION OS

ZOLAI — decision operating system.
Not a product. Not autonomous. Not an AI agent.

ZOLAI is the internal decision operating system behind AXIONA. Its role is not execution, but deterministic decision validation, gating, and audit-grade preparation.

What ZOLAI is

Decision OS
A rule- and invariant-driven decision system where every outcome is explicit and traceable.
Deterministic by design
No learning, no adaptive behavior, no silent changes. Same input always produces the same result.
Guard-first operation
Every decision passes STOP / REVIEW / ALLOW gates. If something cannot be proven, the system halts.
Not UI, not an agent
ZOLAI is not a user interface and not an autonomous AI. Visibility is provided by SENTRA; execution lives elsewhere.

Status

Internal system layer
ZOLAI is internal infrastructure. Not a product, not standardized, not offered commercially, with no ownership or support obligations.
ZOLAI is under continuous, controlled development. The focus is not feature expansion, but refinement of decision logic, guards, and audit-grade guarantees.
