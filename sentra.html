SENTRA
Decision Control Layer

SENTRA is the visible control layer of AXIONA Systems. It provides real-time insight into decisions, reviews, and system state — without executing or modifying anything.

What SENTRA is

Read-only control room
A mission-control style view of decisions, states, and evidence — designed for operational clarity, not demos.
Explicit STOP / REVIEW / AUTO
States are explicit and auditable. No silent change, no implicit behavior.
Traceability
Full decision trace: inputs, rules, evidence, why/why-not — suitable for audit contexts.
No execution logic in the UI
SENTRA visualizes the filesystem-based truth. Execution happens elsewhere — if approved.

What SENTRA is not

Not an automation engine
SENTRA does not run workflows. It does not trigger actions. It does not modify system state.
Not an AI decision maker
No black-box reasoning. Decisions are rule-based, explicit, and reviewable.

Why it exists

Operational control, not hype
SENTRA exists to make complex internal systems controllable: decisions become visible, reviewable, and provable — with deterministic behavior.

Status

Internal pilot / evaluation
This is an internal pilot view. Not a product. Not standardized. No ownership transfer, support, or warranty implied.
