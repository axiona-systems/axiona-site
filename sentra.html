SENTRA — control layer.
Read-only oversight, provable state.

SENTRA is the read-only control layer of AXIONA systems. It exposes states, decisions and runs in an auditable way — without execution.

Explore ZOLAI (internal framework) →
What SENTRA provides
  • Read-only views of state and decisions
  • Auditable event chains
  • Operational transparency
  • Deterministic control points
What it does not do
  • No execution
  • No system modification
  • No silent intelligence
  • No operator replacement

Core principle
